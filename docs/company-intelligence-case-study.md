# Recruiter Case Study: AI Company Intelligence Platform

## Problem

Analysts, founders, sales teams, and product teams often spend hours researching a company before meetings, applications, partnership discussions, or market analysis. The work is repetitive: read the website, identify the business model, summarize products, infer customers, compare competitors, recommend KPIs, and prepare a report.

## Solution

I built an AI-style company intelligence platform that transforms a company URL into an executive report. The tool crawls a public website, extracts visible text, infers the business model, recommends KPIs, generates SWOT analysis, compares market positioning, and presents the output in a dashboard with export options.

## Business Impact

- Reduces first-pass company research from 2-3 hours to approximately 5 minutes.
- Creates a consistent structure for executive summaries, SWOT, KPIs, and strategic recommendations.
- Helps analysts move from raw web research to stakeholder-ready reporting.
- Demonstrates how AI can support human decision-making without replacing review.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Product thinking and user workflow design
- TypeScript and Next.js application development
- API route design and server-side website fetching
- Structured analytics modelling
- KPI recommendation logic
- Dashboard UX for executives
- Report generation and export workflows
- Clear documentation and architecture communication

## Interview Story

"I wanted a project that connects my analytics background with AI and product thinking. I built an AI Company Intelligence Platform where a user enters a company URL and receives a structured executive report. The project shows how I think about data products: start from a real business workflow, extract messy information, structure it into a reusable model, and present it in a way that stakeholders can act on."
